About US

THM 2024

IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally (CEK), established in 2010, has been a leader in technology and innovation. With 10 societies and 2 affinity groups, we've reached significant milestones, including five consecutive IEEE MGA Regional Exemplary Awards (2018-2022) and multiple Darrel Chong Gold and Bronze awards. Our global standing is reinforced by a high rank in the HPSBCP rankings. We’ve spearheaded notable initiatives like the IEEE International Power and Renewable Energy Conference (IPRECON), India's first student-led IAS technical paper conference. Our flagship event, LUXATHON, has gained national prominence, further elevating IEEE SB CEK’s legacy of excellence.
